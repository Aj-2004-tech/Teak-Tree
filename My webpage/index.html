Teak Tree

The teak tree (Tectona grandis) is a tropical hardwood tree known for its high-quality timber. It is native to South and Southeast Asia and is widely cultivated for its durability, strength, and resistance to pests.

Its wood is golden-brown and becomes more beautiful with age, making it a preferred choice for furniture and shipbuilding.
